Why This Matters

One App. All Your Brands.
Working Together.

Most growers mix equipment from different brands. Their apps don't talk to each other. GrowVPD Pro connects them all.

Mars Hydro Light → Light schedule → Sunrise → Peak → Sunset

Create custom light plans with sunrise & sunset simulation — your lights gradually ramp up in the morning and dim down at night, just like the real sun. Set precise on/off times (18/6 for veg, 12/12 for flower), configure sunrise duration (15–120 min ramp-up), sunset duration, and peak brightness (5–100%). Save multiple named plans (Seedling 40%, Veg 80%, Flower 100%) and switch between them with one tap. Enter times via slider or manually type HH:MM for precision. Control Mars Hydro, Spider Farmer & Vivosun LEDs from one place — each light gets its own independent schedule.

🌅 Sunrise simulation 🌇 Sunset simulation 💡 5-100% dimming 📋 Named plans ⏰ Manual HH:MM input 🔄 Stage presets
AC Infinity Ctrl → VPD drifting? → AC Infinity Fan

Your AC Infinity Controller 69/89 Pro reads its own temperature and humidity probe, calculates live VPD, and tunes the Cloudline fan through the UIS cable to stay inside the target band for each growth stage. Same brand, one signed rule — the controller stays in charge, the app just sets the target.

Any Temp Sensor → Night too cold? → Tuya Plug → Heater

Below 18°C at night? A Tuya smart plug flips a regular oil heater on until the tent recovers, then backs off. Works with whichever sensor you trust — AC Infinity, Bluelab, SwitchBot or a cheap Tuya Zigbee probe — the plug is just the dumb switch in the middle.

Vivosun GrowHub → CO2 low during day? → Vivosun CO2 Outlet

Your Vivosun GrowHub sees CO2 drop under target during the photoperiod, opens a Vivosun smart outlet feeding the CO2 regulator, then closes it once levels recover. The rule only runs with lights on and respects a daily runtime cap so you never dose in the dark.

Bluelab Pulse → pH drifting? → Push Notification

A Bluelab Pulse keeps watching root-zone pH through the Edenic cloud. When it drifts outside your safe range, GrowVPD Pro pushes a notification with the reading and current stage — nutrient correction stays a deliberate human decision, never a silent auto-dose.

Any Sensor → Auto diary → Grow Diary

Sensor readings (temp, humidity, VPD, PPFD, CO2) are logged to your grow diary every 15 minutes. Camera snapshots too. Your diary fills itself.

Tested on Real Grows

We’re at the very beginning and we take testing seriously — every integration is validated on actual hardware in an active grow room, not just on a bench. We’re still building up our test rigs and sourcing gear, and we’re big fans of Mars Hydro, AC Infinity, Spider Farmer, Vivosun, Bluelab and SANlight. If any of these teams ever want to work with us directly, we’d love the conversation. Until then, we test carefully and ship quiet fixes whenever we find rough edges.

Smart automation requires internet (WiFi or mobile data) — devices are controlled through their manufacturer cloud APIs (Tuya Cloud, Mars Hydro MQTT, etc.). All other features in the app work fully offline.
