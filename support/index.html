VPD & Growing

VPD (Vapor Pressure Deficit) measures the drying power of the air around your plants. It's calculated from temperature and humidity. Optimal VPD ensures your plants transpire at the right rate, leading to better nutrient uptake and growth. GrowVPD Pro shows you the ideal VPD zone for each growth stage.

Yes (Pro feature). The app supports BLE temperature/humidity sensors for live VPD readings directly on the chart. Compatible sensors include standard BLE environmental sensors.
