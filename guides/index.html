About the Team

GrowVPD Pro is built by a small team of enthusiast growers and programmers. We read every message and fix bugs fast. This community helps us test with real devices and build what growers actually need.

Help Us Test & Source Hardware

GrowVPD Pro is in its early days and we’re tuning every smart device integration end-to-end. Our test rig is still growing — we can’t own every controller, sensor or light yet, and we’re actively reaching out to brands and distributors for hardware to validate against.

Got a Tuya sensor, Mars Hydro light, AC Infinity controller, Spider Farmer fan, Vivosun GrowHub, Bluelab Pulse or SANlight? Jump on Discord and we’ll walk through it together until it just works. If you’re a brand willing to loan or send hardware for testing, our door is wide open.
