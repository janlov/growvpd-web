About

GrowVPD Pro is the most complete offline grow app — combining VPD chart, grow diary, smart device automation (Tuya, Mars Hydro, AC Infinity, Spider Farmer, Vivosun, Bluelab, SANlight), equipment database (200+ products), nutrient calculator, PPFD guide, and more. Built by a small team of growers and programmers, for growers.

Key Features

  • Interactive VPD heatmap with stage-specific zones
  • Full grow diary from seed to harvest
  • Smart automation for 7 device platforms (cross-brand)
  • Light scheduling with sunrise/sunset simulation
  • Equipment database with 200+ products from 47 brands
  • Nutrient calculator for 69+ fertilizers
  • Disease & pest guide (30+ conditions)
  • 12 grow templates
  • Privacy features (PIN, biometric, stealth mode)
  • Offline-first, no sign-up required

What Makes Us Different

  • Only grow app with cross-brand smart device automation — mix Tuya sensors with Mars Hydro fans and AC Infinity controllers in one rule
  • 15 MB vs 100+ MB competitors — lightweight, fast, no bloat
  • Offline-first, no cloud, no account needed — open the app and start using it immediately
  • 11 languages — most competitors have 1-3
